About Us

Digital Transformation and Integration Solutions Joint Stock Company

Who We Are

DTIS was founded during the digital transformation boom in Vietnam and globally. Our goal is to optimize management and operations for businesses, hotels, and luxury resorts.

With a strong focus on innovation, we integrate the latest technology and comply with government regulations to provide top-tier digital solutions.

Our Mission

To provide seamless and efficient digital transformation solutions that enhance operational excellence in the hospitality sector.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of hospitality-focused digital transformation solutions, recognized for innovation and compliance.

Our Core Values

Innovation, Compliance, Customer Focus, and Continuous Improvement.
